Where the Hanindo Group began.

Company overview

Four decades in
petroleum equipment

PT. Hanindo Citra traces its work back to PT. Sugiron Citra, whose petroleum equipment division began operating in 1987. That division became the embryo of what is now the Hanindo Group — and the reason every company in it still measures itself against the same standard of after-sales support.

Today Hanindo Citra supplies, installs and services the equipment that keeps Indonesia's retail fuel industry running: dispensers, tanks, forecourt automation, and the signage and lighting that go with them. The company also takes on construction work for retail petroleum sites, from consultation and drawings through to the day the station opens.

The same legal entity delivers the group's fire-protection work through its Fire Fighting Department, which means the certifications, management systems and engineering discipline below apply across both.
