PT. Hanindo Citra

The company that started the group.

Hanindo Citra continues the work started by PT. Sugiron Citra — the company that became the embryo of the Hanindo Group when its petroleum equipment division began operating in 1987. Today it supplies, installs and services the equipment that keeps Indonesia's retail fuel industry running.

  • Sales & after-sales service of petroleum equipment and product supply
  • Construction services for the retail petroleum industry — from consultation and drawing through building, until the gas station operates
  • Sign boards and lighting for gas stations
