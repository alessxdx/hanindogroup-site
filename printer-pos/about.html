The hardware behind the counter.

From receipt printers to self-service kiosks, Custom (S.E.A) Gralessando supplies and supports the systems that keep retail, transport and hospitality moving.

Company overview

Custom's partner in South East Asia.

One of the companies of the Hanindo Group, Custom (S.E.A) Gralessando represents Custom — a manufacturer of hardware and software for automation, spanning printing, scanning and reading solutions.

The company is Gralessando (S) Pte Ltd, incorporated in Singapore and trading as Custom (S.E.A). It covers South East Asia — Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand and the Philippines — co-ordinated from the Singapore office.
