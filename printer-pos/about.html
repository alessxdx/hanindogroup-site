Custom (S.E.A) Gralessando

One of the six divisions
of the Hanindo Group.

Custom (S.E.A) Gralessando represents Custom, a manufacturer of hardware and software for automation with printing, scanning and reading solutions. The division runs its own site, which carries the full and current product catalogue.

Incorporated in Singapore as Gralessando (S) Pte Ltd, it covers the South East Asian market — Malaysia, the Philippines and Vietnam are all served through this company, with an office in the Philippines alongside the Singapore base.
