Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co. Ltd

The group's international arm.

Established in 2007 and operating from Shanghai, Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co. Ltd is one of the subsidiaries of the Hanindo Group — an emerging leading Total Solution Provider in the Oil and Gas, Automation, Fuel Management Solutions and Automotive Garage industries in the Asia Pacific region. Its purpose extends beyond the domestic Chinese market: to penetrate the export market to the Asia Pacific, the Middle East, and even as far as Africa.

It draws on a group that began in Jakarta, Indonesia in 1987 and has since developed a strong portfolio of partnership with leading global companies in Petrol Dispensing Pumps, Automatic Tank Gauging, Automation Solutions, Petrol Station Equipments and Automotive Garage Industries.

Markets we serve.

From Shanghai, the company works outward into three export regions. Beyond these, its leadership has planned to expand activities by setting up another branch in Europe — a plan for the future rather than a current location.

Asia Pacific

The company's primary export region, extending the group's total solution capability across the markets nearest its Shanghai base.

Middle East

Supply of petroleum equipment, automation and fuel management solutions to customers across the Middle East.

Africa

The furthest reach of the export programme, taking the group's product portfolio as far as the African market.

Strategic alliance

Transponder Technology, Australia.

Hanindo (Shanghai) International has established a strategic alliance with Transponder Technology, a leading Fuel Management Solution Provider from Australia. The alliance adds to the group's existing portfolio of partnership with leading global companies in Petrol Dispensing Pumps, Automatic Tank Gauging, Automation Solutions, Petrol Station Equipments and Automotive Garage Industries.
