Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co., Ltd

Printing, scanning, and point of sale.

Incorporated in 2007 and open in Shanghai since 2008, Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co., Ltd is the Hanindo Group's company in China, where it goes to market as Hanindo CUSTOM China. It represents Custom, for its automation with printing, scanning and reading solutions — the same business the group runs across South East Asia through Gralessando Pte Ltd.

Within China the company works from two locations: Shanghai & Shenzhen.

  • Professional printingPOS, fiscal, ticket, label and mobile printers.
  • Point of sale & data captureTerminals, touch systems, cash registers, scanners and payment terminals.
  • ScanningDocument scanners and multifunction print-and-scan systems.
