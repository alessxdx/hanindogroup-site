Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co. Ltd

The group's international arm.

Established in 2007, Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co. Ltd is the Hanindo Group's company in China, where it goes to market as Hanindo CUSTOM China. It represents Custom, the manufacturer of hardware and software for automation with printing, scanning and reading solutions — the same business the group runs across South East Asia through Custom (S.E.A) Gralessando, divided by market rather than by trade.

It draws on a group that began in Jakarta, Indonesia in 1987 and has since built a portfolio of partnerships with leading global manufacturers across the industries it serves.

Within China the company works from two locations: its Shanghai base and a second office in Shenzhen.
