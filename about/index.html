Our history

It started with one dream.

Hanindo Group was established out of a single ambition — to lead the petroleum equipment and automotive equipment business in Indonesia.

The group grew out of the petroleum equipment division of PT. Sugiron Citra, which began operating in 1987. That division became the embryo of the Hanindo Group, and expanded into an independent organisation while keeping the same commitment to customer service and to the technology behind it.

Today the group brings together six companies covering equipment supply, station construction, retail and industrial automation, workshop equipment, printing and POS, and fire protection.

Milestones

How we got here.

1987

The petroleum equipment division of PT. Sugiron Citra begins operating in Jakarta. It becomes the embryo of the Hanindo Group.

2007

Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co., Ltd is incorporated, opening export markets in Asia Pacific, the Middle East and Africa. Its Shanghai office follows in 2008.

2024

PT. Hanindo Citra is certified to ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018, issued 8 November 2024.

The group

Specialists under one roof.

Each company is a specialist in its own field. Together they cover a project from permits and construction, through equipment and automation, to service for the life of the asset.

01

PT. Hanindo Citra

Sales and after-sales service of petroleum equipment and product supply, continuing the work started by PT. Sugiron Citra. Also provides construction for the retail petroleum industry, plus sign boards and forecourt lighting, and houses the group's Fire Fighting Department.

Oil & Gas
02

PT. Flowtech Engineering

Fuel station construction. Every aspect is handled in-house: permits, materials, labour, mechanical and electrical installation, and training staff on operation and safety. Also serves the oil and gas industry and automotive garage construction.

Flowtech
03

PT. Hanindo Automotive

Autoshop equipment built on the latest technology, to increase efficiency, productivity and ease of use. Sole distributor for garage equipment covering lifting, diagnostics, emissions testing, tyre service and lubrication.

Automotive
04

PT. Hanindo Automation Solutions

Retail and commercial fuel automation, so stations can be controlled and managed securely and remotely. Covers the AdvanZ® family, fleet and fuel card management, forecourt terminals, fuel management for commercial sites and fleets, and access control.

Automation
05

Custom (S.E.A) Gralessando

Printing, scanning and reading systems, with the hardware and software behind them, from the group's principal Custom. The company is Gralessando (S) Pte Ltd, incorporated in Singapore and part of the Hanindo Group representing Custom (S.E.A). It serves the South East Asian market.

Printer & POS
06

Hanindo (Shanghai) International

The company is Hanindo (Shanghai) International Co., Ltd, incorporated in 2007 and part of the Hanindo Group representing Hanindo CUSTOM China. It works from Shanghai and a representative office in Shenzhen, handling the same Custom printing, scanning and reading business as Gralessando, for the Chinese market.
