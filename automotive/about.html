Our own brands

WELDANN and ATOM.

Alongside the manufacturers we represent, Hanindo Automotive builds and supplies equipment under two brands of our own. We control Weldann and ATOM specification, hold the spare parts, and back them with our own warranty, training and service.

WELDANN

Professional-grade welding and workshop equipment — tyre service, lifts, fluid exchange, brake and diagnostic machines. Built for heavy-duty use with long service life, and supported by warranty, training and after-sales service across South East Asia.

ATOM

Vehicle lifts — mid-rise, in-ground and on-ground scissor lifts and two-post lifts from 3.5 to 4 tonnes. ISO 9001 certified, with CNC-machined cylinders, imported seals and 24 V safety control circuits.
