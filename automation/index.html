PT. Hanindo Automation Solutions

Modernised management
for fuel operations.

The retail and commercial petroleum industry needs management systems that keep pace with how it actually operates. Hanindo Automation Solutions answers that need — helping customers control and manage their gas stations securely and remotely, so a site or a whole network can be run easily, securely, and even remotely: saving time & cost.

  • Secure, remote control and management of gas station operations
  • Accuracy and control maintained across every transaction and site
  • Fuel management for commercial sites and vehicle fleets
  • Garage automation, access control and identification systems

Scope of work

Four areas of automation —
retail, fleet, forecourt and access.

Retail automation

Point of sale, back office and head office systems that bring day-to-day gas station operations under one accurate, auditable structure.

Fleet & fuel cards

Fleet card, fuel card and loyalty card management — controlled fuelling, identified drivers and vehicles, and a clear record of every litre.

Forecourt terminals

Intelligent forecourt terminals and ticket printers that put unattended, self-service transactions on the island itself.

Access & identification

Access control and identification systems that decide who may enter, who may fuel, and what each transaction is attributed to.
