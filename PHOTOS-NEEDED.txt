HANINDO GROUP SITE — PHOTOS NEEDED

EXACT filename into /photos/ and it appears automatically — no code
changes needed. Until then the page shows a styled "Photo needed"
placeholder, so nothing looks broken.

heroes and ~800px for cards. Landscape suits heroes and cards.

(/fire-fighting/photos/) which is already fully populated. Do not
