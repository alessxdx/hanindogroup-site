HANINDO GROUP SITE — PHOTOS NEEDED
==================================================
Every slot below is already wired into a page. Drop a file with the
EXACT filename into /photos/ and it appears automatically — no code
changes needed. Until then the page shows a styled "Photo needed"
placeholder, so nothing looks broken.

Format: JPG for photos, PNG for logos. At least ~1000px wide for
heroes and ~800px for cards. Landscape suits heroes and cards.

Currently in /photos/:  logo.png, hero-sky.jpg, hero-building.png

NOTE: the /fire-fighting/ section has its OWN photos folder
(/fire-fighting/photos/) which is already fully populated. Do not
move those files — that section is finished and references them
relatively.
